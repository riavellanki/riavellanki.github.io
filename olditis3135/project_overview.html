Project Overview

  • The UNC Charlotte Tamil student organization's website serves as a dynamic platform for promoting the club's activities and fostering community engagement. Through regular updates, event listings, and photo galleries, the website keeps members informed about upcoming gatherings, cultural celebrations, and opportunities for involvement. Additionally, features such as blog posts, resources, and forums facilitate ongoing communication and collaboration among members, ensuring a vibrant and connected Tamil community on campus.
  • Intended Users:
    1. Club Members
    2. Prospective Club Members
    3. General Public
  • Website Overview
    1. Home: Gives basic info about club
    2. About Us: Gives in depth information about club and it's history/background
    3. Contact Us: Provides links to social media and other forms of contact like email and a contact form
    4. Upcoming Events: Gives information on upcoming events and meetings
    5. Past Events: Shows prospective members and general public what kind of things we do in the club

Page Design

  • Home Page:
    1. Purpose: Give audience background info about club
    2. Audience: Members, Prospective Members, General Public
    3. Content: Paragraph summary/pitch for club and images
    4. Data: No data is neccessary for this page
    5. Actions: Navigation bar at the top can be used to move through website
  • About Us:
    1. Purpose: Give audience background info about club
    2. Audience: Members, Prospective Members, General Public
    3. Content: Paragraph summary/pitch for club and images of past events. New member form can also be found on this page.
    4. Data: This page will collect new member data
    5. Actions: Navigation bar at the top can be used to move through website
  • Upcoming Events:
    1. Purpose: Inform audience of upcoming events that they may be interested in participating in. This will help club engagement.
    2. Audience: Members, Prospective Members, General Public
    3. Content: Images and descriptions of upcoming events with embedded links for sign up forms
    4. Data: This page will collect data on the people who sign up for events
    5. Actions: Navigation bar at the top can be used to move through website
  • Past Events:
    1. Purpose: Inform audience of past events that they may be interested in learning more about. This will help club engagement.
    2. Audience: Members, Prospective Members, General Public
    3. Content: Images and descriptions of past events with embedded links for similar upcoming events
    4. Data: This page will collect data on the people who click the link for similar events
    5. Actions: Navigation bar at the top can be used to move through website
  • Contact Us:
    1. Purpose: Gives audience a way to reach out incase they had further questions.
    2. Audience: Members, Prospective Members, General Public
    3. Content: Links to social media, GroupMe, and club email. Also a form to fill out with any questions.
    4. Data: This page will collect data on the people who fill out the contact us form
    5. Actions: Navigation bar at the top can be used to move through website

Dynamic Functionality

  • This website will use JavaScript in order to send questions, filled out in Contact Us page to our club email so that we can answer it. This website will also use JavaScript to put new member information into our new member list and send the new member a niner enage invite. I will also use JavaScript to put members information into a spreadhseet when they sign up for an event.
